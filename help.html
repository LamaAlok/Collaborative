Guidelines & Help

General Guidelines

Follow these instructions to make the most of The Bin-Buddy System

Sign Up / Log In:

Create an account using your email or phone number. Log in to access your dashboard and track your services.

Schedule a Pickup:

Go to the Request tab and fill in your pickup details. Choose your preferred date, time, and waste type.

Sort Your Waste:

Visit Waste Categories to learn what is Recyclable, Non-Recyclable, or Decomposable. Sort waste correctly before the pickup.

Check Feedback & Progress:

After requesting, track pickup progress in Request Feedback. Read reports or feedback from the team.

Payments:

Use the Payment section to view billing info and confirm transactions.
